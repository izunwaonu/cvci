Lesson Objectives and Key Language

Introduce CVC words with the middle vowel sound "i." Help children recognize the "i" sound in familiar CVC words. Encourage sound recognition through hands-on and movement-based activities.

Key languages: pig, sit, lip, kit, win, zip, bin, tin
